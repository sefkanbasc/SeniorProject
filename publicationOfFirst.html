Publication of the first issue


We are thrilled to announce the forthcoming release of the inaugural issue of the esteemed journal, "Current Trends in Computing (CTC)" This momentous occasion marks the beginning of a groundbreaking era, providing an essential platform for distinguished researchers and scholars to explore, unravel, and disseminate their revolutionary findings and state-of-the-art investigations in the ever-evolving realm of computing.

We are pleased to share that this first edition will feature not one, not two, but an impressive collection of six brand-new articles. These meticulously curated pieces of research form a remarkable treasure trove of knowledge, showcasing the extraordinary potential and advancements within the field of computing.

Within the pages of this seminal publication, readers will gain exclusive access to cutting-edge concepts, trailblazing methodologies, and innovative theories, as leading experts push the boundaries of computing to unprecedented heights. The diligent efforts of these luminaries have culminated in the creation of a seminal compendium, set to leave an indelible mark on the landscape of contemporary computing research.

As the curtain rises on the inaugural issue of "Current Trends in Computing (CTC)," the research community and enthusiasts alike are invited to embark on this transformative journey. Together, we will witness the dawning of a new era and contribute to the advancement of the ever-evolving field of computing, exploring uncharted territories and unlocking limitless possibilities.

Join us as we celebrate this momentous milestone, and let us collectively embark on a voyage that promises to reshape the frontiers of computing knowledge, foster interdisciplinary collaborations, and inspire generations of researchers to forge ahead in their pursuit of technological excellence.
